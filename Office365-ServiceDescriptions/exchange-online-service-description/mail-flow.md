## <a name="custom-routing-of-outbound-email"></a><span data-ttu-id="4bfbd-111">Routage personnalisé du courrier sortant</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="4bfbd-111">Custom routing of outbound email</span></span>

Exchange Online also supports Address Rewrite, which routes outgoing email through an on-premises gateway that modifies the addresses.
